# Third-party assets

## Menu / hamburger (compact header)

- **Shipped assets**: `frontend/static/icons/menu.png` and `frontend/static/icons/menu-dark.png`, referenced from `frontend/src/lib/components/AppHeader.svelte`.
- **Dark variant**: `menu-dark.png` is produced by inverting the light image while preserving alpha (not a separate third-party file).
- **Attribution**: These PNGs are **project-local** (user-supplied artwork checked into the repo). No third-party license line is required for them unless you replace them with licensed stock; then update this file.

## When you add external assets
